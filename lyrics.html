That Licker

I was born without heart
in the sign of love
i fought against the sign
but nothing was fine
i fought and i fought
untill my strength fall out
i touched the star
and the star sailed away
Oh! I hate this pain
i was lost in a desert of people
the last thing i remember...
me drinking a licker

drinking that licker
made my pain sweeter
drinking that licker
cleaned all my bitter

i climbed on the heightest mountain
but god pushed me while i was drinking from the fountain
i was falling forever...untill i discovered that licker
a small sip and i had wings,
an other sip and i ruled the world

drinking that licker
made my pain sweeter
drinking that licker
cleaned all my bitter

My love killed me

Our love was dark but we had light
we could walk side by side
drinking love all the time.

The earth stood still
when i read the novel of my nightmare
devils made my already broken heart to bleed
hitting with their deadly swords

blood was there but i was not dead
the scythe of destiny ripped my hurt
and gave it to her while
she was turning the lights off

now the dark love
became melancholy and hope
was just a word with no meaning
I died today
my ghost will hunt my dreams
i died today
the black night of the future saw me
my queen...
riding a white horse
and i was dead admiring her
through my grave

i died today and there is no resurrection for me
she threw on me 5 roses and a daisy
but when they came to me
there were tails from a whip
i died today and there is no resurrection for me
