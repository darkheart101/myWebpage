A little about me

Ok, some words about me. My name in this world is Theodoros. It is a Greek name and means "gift by god". Anyway I dont like people to call me Theodoros or even Thodoris which is the shorter version of my name because i am an atheist and i have nothing to do with something that - in my opinion - does not exist. I prefer to be called Kouleris (my last name) or even better darkheart (with a lowercase d) which is my nickname. The nickname darkheart came by my pesimist way of thinking. My native language is Greek but I can speak English very well too. I learned English when I was a kid by watching a lot of cartoons on the T.V..

I was born in 1982 on 22nd of September and astrologists say that my sign is virgo. I really have the most of the characteristics of a virgo but what i really am is an INTJ. I was never a normal kid. I was a geek from the fist time I was born. I was passing my time by watching cartoons, reading comic books and playing video games.You might say that those things are something that every child. Indeed, but when you become an adult and you still do the same things is not ordinary. Anyway, I had my first video game console when i was 7 years old. It was an Atari 2600. After that i bought some more video game consoles like Game Boy, Famicom(the japanese version of NES) and Sega Mega Drive(Sega Genesis for the US people). Sadly i don't have any of those now. In the year 2010 i managed to buy a Sony PSP and an Xbox360.

My first computer was bought in 1997 and my life change dramaticaly since then. That year I decided that I want to go to a University that I can learn more about computers. And I did it. In 2000 i went to Technological Education Institute of Lamia where I learned various things not only by the lessons that i attended but also by the people that I met there. I worked as an administrator of the computer lab in the Nurse school and after that I worked for 6 months as a Network technical support staff in Compuforce ltd.

The next important stop of my life was when I went for military training. Those may were hard times because of the small amount of sleep that we were getting but great times because all the fun i had with the other soldiers. After my military training i started working again in Compuforce ltd until the end of July 2007 when i resigned. For a short period of time i teached networks, data bases, microprocessors and linux at Delta Vocational Training Institute. Now I work at Cocoon Kanellopoulos.
